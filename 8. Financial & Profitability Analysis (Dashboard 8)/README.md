## Overview

The **Financial & Profitability Analysis Dashboard** provides an executive-level financial performance view within a global e-commerce business model inspired by Amazon. Built using a synthetic dataset of 100,000 transaction-level records, this dashboard focuses on revenue generation, cost monitoring, gross profit analysis, margin performance, discount impact, and customer segment profitability.

The objective of this dashboard is to enable executive leadership, finance teams, and commercial decision-makers to monitor financial health and profitability drivers across product categories, customer segments, and sales channels.

This dashboard answers critical financial questions:

- What is the total revenue and gross profit generated?
- How efficient is the cost structure (COGS and fulfilment cost)?
- What is the overall gross margin performance?
- Which product categories drive higher profitability?
- Are Prime customers more profitable than Non-Prime customers?
- What is the financial impact of discounts?
- How concentrated is revenue across product categories?

By combining executive KPI monitoring, trend analytics, margin analysis, and interactive filtering, this dashboard delivers a comprehensive financial intelligence solution.

## Business Storyline & Analytical Flow

### 1. Executive Financial Snapshot – KPI Cards

Eight KPI cards provide an immediate summary of financial performance:

- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)**
- **Total Gross Profit**
- **Gross Margin %**
- **Total Orders**
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**
- **Total Fulfilment Cost**
- **Total Discount Impact**

These KPIs establish visibility into revenue scale, cost structure, margin efficiency, and pricing impact.

---

### 2. Revenue vs Cost vs Profit Trend – Line & Clustered Column Chart

- **X-Axis:** Month Name  
- **Column Values:** Total Revenue, Total COGS  
- **Line Value:** Total Gross Profit  

This visual highlights financial performance trends over time, margin expansion or compression, and revenue-to-cost relationships.

---

### 3. Gross Margin % by Product Category – Clustered Bar Chart

- **Axis:** Product Category  
- **Value:** Gross Margin %

This visual identifies high-margin and low-margin product categories, supporting pricing strategy and cost control decisions.

---

### 4. Profitability by Customer Segment – Clustered Column Chart

- **Axis:** Customer Segment (Prime / Non-Prime)  
- **Values:** Total Revenue, Total Gross Profit  
- **Tooltip:** Gross Margin %

This visual evaluates revenue contribution and profit efficiency across customer segments.

---

### 5. Interactive Filtering Controls – Slicers

All visuals dynamically respond to the following slicers:

- Year  
- Month Name  
- Product Category  
- Customer Segment  
- Sales Channel  

These controls allow dynamic financial analysis across time, customer groups, products, and channels.

---

### 6. Gross Margin % Performance – Gauge

- **Value:** Gross Margin %  
- **Target:** 35%  

Provides a clear executive view of whether margin performance meets strategic targets.

---

### 7. Revenue Distribution by Product Category – Pie Chart

- **Legend:** Product Category  
- **Values:** Total Revenue  

Displays revenue concentration and diversification across product categories.
