## Overview

The **Forecasting & Scenario Modelling Dashboard** delivers a forward-looking analytical view within a global e-commerce business model inspired by Amazon. Built using a synthetic dataset of 100,000 transaction-level records, this dashboard focuses on revenue trends, growth projections, fulfilment cost monitoring, discount sensitivity, and performance benchmarking against strategic targets.

Unlike traditional historical dashboards, this solution emphasizes predictive thinking and scenario evaluation, enabling leadership teams to assess future growth potential, profitability sustainability, and operational efficiency under changing business conditions.

This dashboard answers key forward-looking business questions:

- What is the current revenue trajectory?
- What is the projected revenue outlook?
- Are we on track to meet growth targets?
- How do discounts influence revenue performance?
- How stable is gross margin over time?
- How concentrated is revenue across product categories?
- Is fulfilment cost aligned with growth expectations?

Through KPI monitoring, trend analysis, category segmentation, interactive slicers, and performance gauges, this dashboard supports strategic forecasting and scenario-driven decision-making.

---

## Business Storyline & Analytical Flow

### 1. Executive Forecast Snapshot – KPI Cards

Eight KPI cards provide an immediate executive-level summary:

- **Total Revenue**
- **Total Fulfilment Cost**
- **Revenue Forecast (3 Months)**
- **Forecast Growth %**
- **Total Orders**
- **Average Order Value (AOV)**
- **Gross Margin %**
- **Average Discount %**

These KPIs establish visibility into scale, growth assumptions, operational cost structure, and profitability efficiency.

---

### 2. Revenue Trend – Area Chart

- **X-Axis:** Date (Continuous)
- **Y-Axis:** Total Revenue

This visual highlights historical revenue trajectory and seasonality patterns, forming the foundation for forecasting analysis.

---

### 3. Gross Margin Trend – Line Chart

- **X-Axis:** Month
- **Y-Axis:** Gross Margin %

Tracks margin stability over time and identifies volatility or compression risk.

---

### 4. Revenue by Product Category – Clustered Column Chart

- **Axis:** Product Category
- **Value:** Total Revenue

Identifies revenue concentration and category dependency risk, supporting growth sensitivity analysis.

---

### 5. Discount vs Revenue Trend – Line & Clustered Column Chart

- **X-Axis:** Month Name
- **Column Value:** Total Revenue
- **Line Value:** Average Discount %

Evaluates whether revenue growth is increasingly dependent on discounting, helping assess pricing sustainability.

---

### 6. Forecast Target Achievement – Gauge

- **Value:** Total Revenue
- **Target:** Forecast Target (Revenue + 10%)

Provides a clear executive benchmark for growth target performance.

---

### 7. Interactive Filtering Controls – Slicers

The dashboard includes twelve interactive slicers:

- Order Date  
- Year  
- Quarter  
- Month  
- Customer Segment  
- Country  
- State  
- Product Category  
- Sub Category  
- Brand  
- Sales Channel  
- Marketing Channel  

All visuals dynamically respond to these filters, enabling multi-dimensional forecasting analysis.
